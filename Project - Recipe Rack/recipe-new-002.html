Veggie Stir-Fry

Prep 5m • Cook 10m • Serves 1

✕

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp garlic paste
  • ½ tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add all vegetables and stir on high flame.
  3. Add soy sauce, garlic, chili, and salt.
  4. Cook until veggies are slightly soft but crunchy.
  5. Serve with rice or noodles.
