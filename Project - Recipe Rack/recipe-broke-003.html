Egg Fried Rice

Prep 5m • Cook 10m • Serves 1–2

✕

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1–2 tbsp oil
  • Soy sauce, salt, pepper
  • Optional: vegetables or spring onions

Method

  1. Heat oil, scramble eggs and set aside.
  2. Sauté onion (and veg if using) until soft.
  3. Add rice and soy sauce; toss well.
  4. Mix in scrambled eggs, season to taste and garnish with spring onions.
