Cheese Burst Pizza

Prep 20m • Cook 15–18m • Serves 2–3

✕

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella and processed cheese (for the burst)
  • Toppings: pepperoni/veggies as desired
  • Olive oil, oregano, chili flakes

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 240°C (hot oven).
  2. Roll dough, spread sauce and layer generous cheese.
  3. Add toppings and bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
  4. For extra burst, add processed cheese pockets before baking.
