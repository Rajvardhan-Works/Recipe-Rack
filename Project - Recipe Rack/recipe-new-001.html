Creamy Garlic Pasta

Prep 5m • Cook 15m • Serves 2

✕

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup milk or cream
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup grated cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Method

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in butter until golden.
  3. Add milk/cream and simmer.
  4. Mix in cheese and stir until creamy.
  5. Toss pasta into the sauce and serve hot.
