Classic Tiramisu

Prep 30m • Chill 2h • Serves 4

✕

Ingredients

  • 200g mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1 cup coffee
  • 10+ ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup sugar

Method

  1. Whip mascarpone and sugar until smooth.
  2. Fold in whipped cream.
  3. Dip biscuits in coffee and layer them.
  4. Spread cream mixture on top.
  5. Repeat layers and dust with cocoa.
  6. Refrigerate for 2 hrs before serving.
