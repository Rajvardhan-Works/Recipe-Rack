Quinoa Power Bowl

Prep 15m • Cook 20m • Serves 2

✕

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed)
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • 1 cup roasted sweet potato cubes
  • 1 cup chickpeas (roasted)
  • Handful spinach or kale
  • Olive oil, lemon, salt, pepper

Method

  1. Cook quinoa in water/broth until fluffy (≈15 min).
  2. Toss roasted sweet potato and chickpeas with a little oil and salt.
  3. Assemble bowl: quinoa base, add veggies, greens and chickpeas.
  4. Drizzle lemon and olive oil, season to taste, serve warm.
