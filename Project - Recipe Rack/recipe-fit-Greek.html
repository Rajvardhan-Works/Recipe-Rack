Greek Yogurt Bowl

Prep 5m • Serves 1

✕

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp granola or nuts
  • Optional: chia seeds or cinnamon

Method

  1. Spoon Greek yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Top with berries, honey and granola.
  3. Add seeds or cinnamon as desired; serve immediately.
