Chocolate Lava Cake

Prep 10m • Bake 12–14m • Serves 4

✕

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs + 2 egg yolks
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup flour
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together and let cool slightly.
  2. Whisk eggs, yolks and sugar until pale.
  3. Fold chocolate mixture and sifted flour into the eggs.
  4. Pour into greased ramekins and bake at 200°C for 12–14 minutes.
  5. Invert onto plates and serve immediately with ice cream.
