Masala Maggie

Prep 2m • Cook 5m • Serves 1

✕

Ingredients

  • 1 packet instant noodles (maggie)
  • 1½ cups water
  • Vegetables (onion, tomato, peas) — optional
  • 2 tbsp oil, salt & spices
  • Masala packet (from the noodles)

Method

  1. Heat oil, sauté onions/tomato quickly (optional).
  2. Add water and bring to boil.
  3. Add noodles and cook 2 minutes, then add masala and vegetables.
  4. Stir and cook until water reduces to desired consistency. Serve hot.
