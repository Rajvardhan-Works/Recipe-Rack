One-Pot Egg Rice

Prep 5m • Cook 10m • Serves 1–2

✕

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • ½ tsp soy sauce
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan; scramble eggs and keep aside.
  2. Sauté onions until slightly golden.
  3. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Mix in scrambled eggs.
  5. Cook for 2–3 minutes, garnish with spring onions.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy.
