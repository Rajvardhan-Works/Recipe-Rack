Lemon Grilled Fish

Prep 10m • Cook 12–15m • Serves 2

✕

Ingredients

  • 2 fish fillets (tilapia, salmon or similar)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt, pepper, fresh herbs

Method

  1. Season fish with salt, pepper, paprika and lemon juice.
  2. Brush with olive oil and grill 6–8 minutes per side (depending on thickness).
  3. Finish with fresh herbs and an extra squeeze of lemon. Serve hot.
