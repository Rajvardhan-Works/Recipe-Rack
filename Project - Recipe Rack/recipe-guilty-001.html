Loaded Fries

Prep 10m • Cook 15m • Serves 2

✕

Ingredients

  • 400g frozen or fresh fries
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar/mozzarella)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce (optional)
  • Chopped spring onions, jalapeños, bacon bits (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

Method

  1. Cook fries according to package or oven-bake until crispy.
  2. Arrange fries on a baking tray, sprinkle cheese and bacon bits.
  3. Bake 3–5 minutes until cheese melts.
  4. Mix mayo and hot sauce; drizzle over fries and top with onions & jalapeños.
