Bread Pizza

Prep 5m • Cook 8–10m • Serves 1

✕

Ingredients

  • Bread slices (1–2)
  • Tomato ketchup or pizza sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Chopped onion, capsicum, tomato (optional)
  • Oregano/chilli flakes

Method

  1. Spread sauce on bread slices.
  2. Top with veggies and grated cheese.
  3. Bake in oven or toast in a pan until cheese melts and edges are crisp.
  4. Sprinkle oregano or chilli flakes and serve.
