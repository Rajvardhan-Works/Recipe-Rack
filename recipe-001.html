Grilled Chicken Salad

Prep 10m • Cook 15m • Serves 2

✕

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Lemon, salt & pepper

Method

  1. Season chicken and grill 6–8 minutes per side until cooked.
  2. Toss greens and tomatoes with olive oil and lemon.
  3. Slice chicken, place on salad, serve immediately.
