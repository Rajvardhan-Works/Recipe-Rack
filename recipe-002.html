Chocolate Brownie

Prep 10m • Bake 20m • Serves 6

✕

Ingredients

  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. Mix melted butter and sugar until smooth.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla, whisk well.
  3. Sift in flour, cocoa powder, and salt; fold gently.
  4. Pour into a greased baking tray.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 18–20 minutes.
  6. Cool, slice, and serve warm.
